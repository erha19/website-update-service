Introduction
From the day that Weex donated to Apache Incubator, this project started its journey of transformation. Within this past year, it is delighted to see the steady progress on the performance, stability of Weex, and the growth of the adoption and a reviving community ecosystem. On the other hand, there is still room for improving the Apache Way practice and community buildup. So, on January 19th, 2018, we’d like to invite you to this conference to share the understanding of Apache Way, the experience on using or contributing Weex, and discuss the future of this project, to help Weex graduate from Apache Incubator as soon as possible.

Niclas Hedhman - Apache Software Foundation
The Apache Way
The Apache Way is the culture within the Apache Software Foundation, a set of values, principles, guidelines, and to a lesser degree processes, to ensure strong community engagement over long periods of time. The Apache Way is about fostering communities of individuals, where company agendas are pushed to the side and a collaborative spirit is grown in the interest of the community. It is about Community, rather than Code. It is about a life choice, rather than career opportunity. It is about growing an extended family.

Cheng Shaofei (Hanquan) - Senior Technical Expert of Alibaba
The Past, Now and Future of Weex Community
Weex was born in 2015 and open-sourced since 2016, all the Weex community operations are pursuing the Apache Way and open source mentality. During this session we will talk about how Weex community run itself, problems we are facing and progress we've made, including the future of this community we are expecting.

Weex Technical Evolution
Thanks to the upgrade of Weex interactive capabilities and architecture improvements, Weex grows from only supporting '11.11' mega events to the extensive usage across many of the Alibaba businesses during the past year. During this session we are going to share the technical evolvement on Weex components, interactive capabilities, stability and performance, and how Weex face and tackle challenges.

Tang Wei (Youxi) - Senior Engineer of Fliggy
Weex + UI
Fliggy adopted Weex since the October of 2016, during 1 year practice we accumulated a lightweight, high performance and rich interactive UI components library, and we've encapsulated many of the Weex native components and provides them to the frontend developers as an out-of-the-box solution, and this has been fully tested within the '11.11' mega events senarios. During this talk we are going to introduce the 0 to 1 step of the Fliggy Weex ecosystem, share the experience on implementing Weex UI, and hope you can join the open source contribution.
